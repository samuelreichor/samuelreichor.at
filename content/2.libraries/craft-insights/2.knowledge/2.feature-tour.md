## Dashboard

Everything you need, one screen. No clutter, no complexity.

![Insights Dashboard Overview](/images/bitmap/craft-insights-dashboard.png)

Your key metrics front and center: [Pageviews](/libraries/craft-insights/knowledge/how-tracking-works#pageviews), [Unique Visitors](/libraries/craft-insights/knowledge/how-tracking-works#unique-visitors), [Bounce Rate](/libraries/craft-insights/knowledge/how-tracking-works#bounce-rate), [Time on Page](/libraries/craft-insights/knowledge/how-tracking-works#avg-time-on-page), and [Real-time Visitors](/libraries/craft-insights/knowledge/how-tracking-works#real-time-visitors). Trend indicators show growth or decline compared to the previous period.
